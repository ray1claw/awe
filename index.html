Augmented-reality Widget Engine
What?
Awe is an emotion comparable to wonder but less joyous, and more fearful or respectful. Awe or "augmented-reality widget engine" aims at bringing 3D widgets snapped to reality, so you can never lose those sticky notes on the door, or live weather information on your table! Virtual widgets all around you keep you updated on meta-information all the time. Works (best) with an HMD.
